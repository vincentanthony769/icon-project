ICONIC: Beyoncé's Renaissance Tour by the Numbers

Surpassing half a billion in gross, "Queen Bey" reigns with the top-earning tour by a woman.

October 13, 2023

Credit: Vincent Anthony

By Vincent Anthony

With her first solo tour since 2016, Beyoncé has broken records yet again thanks to this year’s "Renaissance World Tour.” Kicking off in Stockholm, Sweden on May 10, the 56-stop stadium trek began in Europe before landing stateside in July, ending on October 1st in Kansas City. The extravagantly opulent production echoed the album’s larger-than-life energy, which had radiating effects not only on Beyoncé’s stratospheric superstar status but also on the United States’ economy. The New York Times reports that the tour generated an estimated $4.5 billion dollars, on par with the effect of the 2008 Olympics on Beijing.

Let's take a look at the "Renaissance World Tour" by the numbers.

Beyoncé performed 56 sold-out stadium shows on her "Renaissance World Tour," stopping in 39 cities throughout Europe and North America. It’s her longest trek since her 132-stop arena tour, “The Mrs. Carter Show World Tour,” in 2013 and 2014.

Across the tour's 56 stops, over 2.7 million tickets were sold to Beyoncé's adoring "BeyHive." This total number of attendees is the largest of any Beyoncé tour to date. The veteran diva, who debuted in 1997 as the lead singer of R&B girl-group Destiny's Child, has certainly come a long way. Her first stateside solo tour in 2004 was a co-headlined alongside Alicia Keys and Missy Elliott and sold 280k tickets, grossing $19 million across 30 shows.

The "Renaissance World Tour" grossed a total revenue of $592.4 million – more than her last two tours combined, which each made 49 stadium stops. The average ticket price was $213.

Beyoncé's "Renaissance World Tour" is now the highest-grossing concert tour by a woman in music and is top ten overall. Next, she’s set her sights on another box office: movie theaters. “RENAISSANCE: A Film by Beyoncé,” a documentary about the tour, opens in theaters on December 1 and is poised for another record-breaking gross.
